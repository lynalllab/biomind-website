Aisling is a PhD student in the Department of Psychiatry, University of Cambridge, funded by the DTP MRC iCASE programme. She graduated from University College Dublin with a B.Sc. in Neuroscience in 2025. She is researching the molecular and health effects of genetic risk for psychiatric disorders, in partnership with the data federation company [Bitfount](https://www.bitfount.com). Beyond scientific research, she competes for Ireland in women's épée.
