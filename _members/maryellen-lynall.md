Mary-Ellen is a neuroimmunologist and practising psychiatrist who leads the BioMind Lab, working between the University of Cambridge and University of Oxford. She is also co-founder and co-chair of the [Psychiatric Genomics Consortium Functional Genomics Workgroup](https://pgc.unc.edu/for-researchers/working-groups/functional-genomics-working-group/), an international hub for large-scale, open science. She serves as blood biomarker lead for the [UKRI Mental Health Platform](https://www.mentalhealthplatform.ac.uk). She hails from Belfast and outside the lab, enjoys learning languages, music, and team workout training.
